📍 Entry Triggers (Priority Order)
#1 — Liq Sweep + BOS (PRIME)
Price sweeps liquidity, then closes through structure. Enter on BOS close. Stop just beyond the sweep wick.
#2 — Order Block (SECONDARY)
Missed the sweep entry. Price retraces into the OB zone. Stop still above/below the original liq sweep.
#3 — FVG or Equilibrium (BACKUP)
Enter at 50% of the FVG or the 50% equilibrium level. Stop above recent high (downtrend) or below recent low (uptrend).
Structure Bias
Uptrend → CALLS · Downtrend → PUTS · Ranging → lower confidence, both sides
🎯 Take Profit System
TP1
Take 50% off position at first liquidity zone. Move stop to entry — trade is now risk-free.
TP2
Take 25% off remaining at next liquidity zone. Trail stop below new high (longs) or above new low (shorts).
TP3
Close the remaining 25%. Shut trading apps. Walk away. You are done for the day.
Target zones: Highs for longs (calls) · Lows for shorts (puts)
💰 Risk Management
Max Risk Per Trade
1% of total account — non-negotiable
Contract Sizing Formula
Contracts = floor(Account × 1%) ÷ (entry price × 100)
Stop Loss
Place stop just above/below the liquidity sweep wick — not arbitrary
Never Do This
No overleverage · No revenge trading · No chasing · No adding to losers
🧠 Psychology Rules
Think Like a Child
Keep it simple. 1-3 steps max. No "what ifs". No overcomplicating. The strategy is the strategy.
After a Win
Be emotionless. You have an edge. Wins are expected — don't get hyped, don't trade again.
After a Loss
Be okay with it. You won't win every trade. Log it, learn from it, close the apps.
You Trade Probability
Not hope. Strategy + risk management = profitable over time. Trust the process. Long game only.
